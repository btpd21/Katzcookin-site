About Spencer

With a foundation in fine dining and a certification as Chef de Cuisine, Spencer Katz combines technical excellence with extraordinary versatility. Drawing from over a decade of experience preparing pescatarian, vegan, vegetarian, dairy-free, and histamine-sensitive dishes, he specializes in creating meals that are thoughtful, refined, and deeply personal. Whether it’s aging and curing meats or crafting globally inspired plant-based plates, Spencer brings warmth, professionalism, and precision to every bite.

Services

  • Private dinners and tasting menus
  • Event and retreat catering
  • Personalized weekly meal prep
  • Late-night and pop-up menu creation
  • Custom dietary accommodation (pescatarian, vegan, dairy-free, etc.)
  • Smoking, curing, and aged meat presentations
